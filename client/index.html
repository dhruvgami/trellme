TrellMe is a notification and reporting service for your Trello boards.

Features

Daily Reports

Keep up with your team's activities by getting a full view of activity across all your Trello boards, every day. Reports include cards due now, upcoming and past due. They also include full snapshots of selected boards.

Notifications

Email alerts sent out when cards are due. This helps make sure you don't miss any deadlines and stay on top of your commitments.

Inform Others

Coming Soon
Automate activity reporting by creating custom reports and notification profiles. Eliminate additional workload of putting together communication to inform others.
